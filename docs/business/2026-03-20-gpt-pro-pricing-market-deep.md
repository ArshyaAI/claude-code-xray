# Key Numbers

## Swiss Market Reality
- 624,219 SMEs total, but 90% are micro (1-9 employees)
- Real addressable: 28,845 firms (20-249 employees)
- Near-term SAM (15-25% of those): 3,780-6,300 firms
- SAM value: CHF 155M-422M
- SOM (capacity-limited): 12-15 clients = CHF 492K-1M first year

## Current Pricing Is Too Low
- CHF 3-5K/mo retainer = only 13-33 hours at Swiss specialist rates
- CHF 5-7K setup = only 22-47 hours (not enough for bespoke)
- Swiss comparables: CHF 150-230/h, even a 1-day workshop sells at CHF 1,650

## Recommended Pricing
- Discovery/ROI sprint: CHF 2-5K
- Implementation: CHF 12-30K+ (fixed fee)
- Operate retainer: CHF 1.5-6K/mo
- No revenue share. No per-agent pricing.

## Positioning
- Kill: "AI transformation partner for Swiss SMEs"
- Better: "We build AI operating systems for Swiss brands that cut back-office workload in 30 days"
- Or: "Swiss-made AI workflows for teams drowning in multilingual operations"

## ICP (from NIKIN signal)
Swiss consumer brands / e-commerce, 20-100 employees, multilingual ops, heavy content, back-office complexity

## Timing
- 22% → 34% AI adoption in Swiss SMEs (2024→2025)
- 37% still experimenting, need external help
- Main hurdle: pilot → company-wide scaling (exactly our wedge)
